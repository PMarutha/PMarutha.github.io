Stock Market Simulator



Stock Market Simulator is a simulated "real-time" graph of the value of one of three stocks (GME, AMC, and BBW).
Users can choose to spend their money on buying more stocks, which causes the value of the stock on the graph to increase.
If a user does not buy stocks, the value steadily depreciates, being reflected on the graph as well.
At the end of the game, the game reports the net loss or gain the user accumulated through buying stocks.

Hangman Game

Hangman is a game coded using Turtle in Python that simulates the popular children's word guessing game.
A word is chosen from a predefined list of words and users are prompted to guess letters.
If the user correctly guesses a letter present in the word, the letters are written on the corresponding spaces of the word. If not, the incorrect letter is added to the bottom of the screen and a body part is added to the man.
If the user guesses all the letters of the word before all the man's body parts are drawn, they win!

Musical Frogs E-Card



Musical Frogs is an e-card designed to brighten the lives of children at a local children's hospital
Users can start by clicking on a lilypad to open up their e-card.
They then can click on a frog to advance it across the screen from lilypad to lilypad, towards the goal of reaching the final message.
The end screen displays a get well soon message and several frogs. Users can click on each frog, which plays different musical notes.
